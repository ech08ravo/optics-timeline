Options & Considerations

Factor Closed / Proprietary Open / Transparent Why It Matters
Data Ownership Meta, Apple, Google — data harvested Brilliant Labs, OpenGlass — local/user-owned Privacy, research ethics, student data
Processing Cloud (latency, dependency) On-device (private, limited) Reliability, GDPR, institutional policy
Validation Marketing claims, no peer review Research-backed, published methods Educational integrity, evidence-based
Regulatory Status Consumer electronics (unregulated) Medical-grade (FDA/CE cleared) Liability, duty of care, health claims
